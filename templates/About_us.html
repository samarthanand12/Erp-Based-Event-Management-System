About Us

About the Institute

MIET, located in the National Capital Region, is one of the oldest self-financed Engineering Institutions in Uttar Pradesh and also one of the best Skill Enhancement Training centers. MIET is declared a STAR PERFORMER (an Academic Excellence Award by UPTU). Like a true leader, it has taken several initiatives in the field of technical education that are being followed by other Institutions across the state now. MIET has an intensive academic environment aimed at bringing out the best out of the students. It lays claim to the finest faculty in the region, offers the best laboratory infrastructure, excellent hostels, mess facilities and promotes several extra- curricular activities.

About Our Technical Committee

We at Udyat, are a team of enthusiastic, highly dynamic and motivated young budding engineers, who are dedicated to make every student an expert. We are a collective group formed out of the senior students of MIET(CSE). With an enlightened mind and the will to learn, teach and share our knowledge with others, we people at Udyat give our present constantly to make a brighter future for ourselves and our fellow Means. Udyat is a team that specializes in the computer science and information technology domain. Thus, we people are the computer science society of Miet. We specializes in almost all domains of the field including python, Salesforce, Java, machine learning, android, web development, etc.
